# **"Virtual Pipelines" Transport Natural Gas Across the Country**

## For this project, I used three.js, Flourish, R and Procreate to make and stylize graphs as well as animate drawings I made. I wanted to try using Flourish at least once, and used it to make 5 heated tree maps (3 of which I included). I used three.js to add 3d elements such as the "glitch" effect and slight shadows behind some of the text. I used Procreate to animate and draw elements to go along with my story, mostly since there are so few visuals of virtual pipelines on the internet.

## Data wise, I took data from the EIA and wrote a traditional news story with multiple news pegs, integrating data, drawing and scrollytelling to guide the reader through the history, uses and harms of virtual pipelines. I also used R, mainly, to parse the data as well as to make the graphs before editing them in Adobe Illustrator and exporting them with ai2html.
